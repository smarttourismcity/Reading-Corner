About Us

Reading Corners provide a quiet space for students to read, study, and collaborate. Equipped with books, comfortable seating, and a conducive atmosphere, they are perfect for focused learning.

Explore Now

Diponegoro University offers several reading corners, located in its main library, faculty libraries, and open spaces, promoting a culture of reading and research among students. The objectives of creating the Reading Corner are:

  • Events for regular book discussions, workshops, and reading campaigns are organized to foster student engagement and creativity.
  • Promoting to inspire students and visitors to utilize the Reading Corners as a space for reading, studying, or discussions.
  • Providing to deliver comprehensive information about the Reading Corners at Diponegoro University, including their locations, facilities, and available activities.

Further Explanation About Reading Corner

Additional Information About Reading Corner

  • 01 What facilities are available at the reading corner?

    The reading corner is equipped with comfortable seating, adequate lighting, and shelves filled with a wide range of books and periodicals. There are also charging stations available for electronic devices.

  • 02 Who can access the reading corner? Are there any specific requirements for students or the public?

    The reading corner is accessible to both students and the general public. Students can use their ID cards to enter, while members of the public may need to register for access.

  • 03 Is the reading corner an indoor or outdoor space? What is the atmosphere like inside?

    The reading corner is an indoor space with climate control for comfort throughout the day. It is designed to offer a peaceful atmosphere for reading, with natural light flowing through large windows.
